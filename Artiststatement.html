Artist Statement

My practice focuses on exploring intuitive figuration from daily life. I experiment with different materials, looking forward to discovering happenings that my work brings to me, rather than just mimicking images from the reality and copy or collage them. Painting and drawing is my major, while installations, design, performance … all would be my media for artistic expression, because I believe there is no real boundary in art. I see painting and drawing as the basis of my practice. I want my painting would like my perception, which is shuttling in the space. Therefore, my paintings are not superflat, it would be looser and more abstract; except for some basic elements in painting like subjects, brushwork, composition etc., elements working in the space like volume, thickness, speed etc. are what I am more interested in. The same is true of other works I make. I am always excited about the journey to the unknown destination. I hope my viewers can be more a traveler or a thinker, and I hope my perception would bring them different thinking in their daily life.

@Jiachen Zeng
